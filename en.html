BASM


Who is BASM? The Bucharest Applied STEAM Museum. STEAM – Science, Technology, Engineering, Art, Math – is a methodology which explains many of the connections in the world we live in. A world in which everyone can learn anything. We believe in the unspoken magic of science centers and that Bucharest needs a modern one now, more than ever. That's why we decided to build one.

Mission! To engage curiosity, smudge T-Shirts, blister fingertips... through the intersection of Science, Technology, Engineering, Art & Math, through community tinkering, free experimentation, and fearless inquiry.

POCKET LAB? A gadget developed by us, BASM. Pocket Lab brings science discovery into every student's pencil box and transforms learning into an active experience inspired by their interests.

But we can't do any of that alone.
If you think we could all grow stronger by teaming up, we'd love to hear from you.
